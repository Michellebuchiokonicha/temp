Landmark Waterview

The Landmark Waterview Apartments is your opportunity to live at the fastest-growing business, leisure, and lifestyle destination in West Africa, Landmark Lagos. With commanding views of the Atlantic and the Lagos skyline, the Landmark Waterview apartments is a bold new vision for urban living in the heart of Victoria Island. This proposed exclusive residential development offers 28 floors of exquisite apartments and 4 floors of secure indoor parking with an attractive retail high street below. Residents can relish in the myriad of world-class facilities, There is a choice of 306 apartments comprising of studios, and a range of 1 to 4-bedroom apartments and penthouses all delivered in white-box finish. Immerse yourself with an array of amenities and activate your lifestyle with Waterview.
